Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help people find information about players who have marked an era in the history of soccer, to know about their statistics and also to help them know which player was the best in history. On this page you can also find the teams in which these players played and some basic statistics about them.

Audience

Soccer can give us many joys. It is these joys that we want to transmit through this website. The audience is people who want to learn more about players who have made history in soccer, as well as important teams and players.

Content

Home page

Home page will have a navigation bar where it will have the logo on it. It will have a paragraph that explains what the web page is about. It will have some pictures according to the web page. The navigation bar will have two options to check more information about soccer players.

Images for the Home page

New Generation

This new generation page will have five soccer players that became the greatest in their generation. It will also have some background underneath their pictures, which includes where they are from, what they won, what they did with their own countries, and clubs.

Images for New Generation

Old Generation

On Old Generation page, it will be given 5 more pictures of those who conquered soccer in their generation. They will also have information about their background, what did they achieve, and their statistics.
